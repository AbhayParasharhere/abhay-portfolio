  14. PNP & IMMIGRATION REFERENCE — BC TECH PILOT

This section is for your reference only — not for resumes. Know this before every offer conversation.

What You Need for BC PNP
	•	NOC TEER Category: 0, 1, 2, or 3 — Software developers are NOC 21232 (TEER 1) ✓
	•	Wage threshold: must meet or exceed the BC median wage for that NOC (~$38–45/hr for software developer — verify current rate at WorkBC before each offer)
	•	Full-time: minimum 30 hours/week
	•	Must have a valid job offer from a BC employer (or qualifying Canadian employer)
	•	PGWP: graduate from VIU (eligible DLI) → apply for PGWP after convocation → length tied to program length (5-year degree = 3-year PGWP maximum)
What to Check on Every Offer
	•	Is the company registered in BC? (BC Business Registry)
	•	Does the wage meet the current NOC median? (check WorkBC at offer time)
	•	Is the role clearly described as 'software developer' or equivalent TEER 1 NOC?
	•	Is it full-time permanent (not contract)? Contract roles are harder for PNP
	•	Ask HR directly: 'Does the company support LMIA or PNP applications for international hires?' — ask after verbal offer, before signing
Roles That Qualify (NOC TEER 1)
	•	Software Developer / Software Engineer — NOC 21232 ✓
	•	Web Developer — NOC 21233 ✓
	•	Database Analyst — NOC 21223 ✓
	•	Business Systems Analyst — NOC 21221 ✓
	•	Data Scientist / Data Engineer — NOC 21211 ✓
Roles That Do NOT Qualify — Avoid
	•	QA Tester (TEER 2 — lower wage threshold, still OK but verify)
	•	IT Support / Help Desk (TEER 2/3 — lower threshold)
	•	Any body-shop consulting firm that 'places' you — unclear employer of record
	•	Contract / temp positions — much harder to use for PNP
Timeline Reality
	•	Graduate March 2026 → convocation April/May 2026 → apply PGWP immediately after
	•	PGWP processing: 2–16 weeks (apply before current study permit expires)
	•	BC PNP: can apply once you have 12 months of qualifying work experience
	•	You can work on your study permit after graduation while PGWP is processing (check current IRCC rules)
	•	Charvar Networks job does NOT qualify for PNP — noted, correct to be looking for qualifying role
